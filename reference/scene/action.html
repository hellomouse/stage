> - Action

The Action character inserts the text following it in Stage's output as a clickable "action".
Actions are the main way users are expected to interact with Stage plays. The most simple form of an action is the "blocking" action, which stops the play until an action is clicked, the second form which is more used is the "non-blocking" action. "Non-blocking" actions take a Subscene and do not stop the play.
