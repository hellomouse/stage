Editor

The editor has three panels: left, middle, and right.

Left panel

The left panel has the scenes tab, variables tab, and play metadata/info tab.
In the scenes tab you can see all scenes in the current play, you can add new scenes by pressing the New button at the top of the middle panel.
In the variables tab, you can see the values of current variables in the play, useful for debugging.
In the info tab, you can set the name and author of the play, this is required for exporting the play.

Middle panel

The middle panel houses the text area where you write your scenes. At the bottom of the panel the five most recently viewed scenes can be seen, the four most recent scenes can be selected with the hotkeys ctrl+1 to ctrl+4, with ctrl+1 being most recent, while the text area is selected.
At the top of the panel are the buttons new, run, start, save, export, and import; these respectively create a new scene, run the play from the currently selected scene, run the play from the "start" scene, save all scenes, export the scene as a .play file, import a .play file.

Right panel

The right panel has the output and help tabs, you are currently viewing the help tab.
Output shows the output from the play as would be seen if you gave Stage a .play file.
